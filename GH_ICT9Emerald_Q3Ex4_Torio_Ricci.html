The Top 4 Gymnasts of 2023



1. Simone Biles
2. Kohei Uchimura
3. Sam Mikulak
4. Dipa Karmakar
5. Shang Chunsong
Simon Biles is said to be the best gymnast of all time and is an icon in American Gymnastics. She has a net worth of $16 Million. Kohei Uchimura is nicknamed "Superman" and owns multiple consecutive titles. He has a net worth of $5 Million. Aside from a few slip-ups in competitions, Sam Mikulak has a very impressive track record since he has won multiple championships. Mikulak has a net worth of $1 Million. Dipa Karmakar became the first ever Indian person to qualify for and compete in the Olympics. It is possible that she is the reason for gymnastics being one of India's most popular sports. Karmakar has a net worth of $8 million. Shang Chunsong is said to be better at the all-around podium than most American gymnasts. Chunsong has a net worth of $10 million.
